Email Template – Southern Partners Fund

Project Description

Southern Partners Fund, a non-profit based out of Atlanta, needed help with an updated email template so that they’d be able to send out their email newsletter and any additional announcements. I designed and built the template within Constant Contact, ensuring that the template would be user friendly so that their team can go in, edit, and send out emails themselves while also being mobile-responsive and staying compliant with any necessary laws.
